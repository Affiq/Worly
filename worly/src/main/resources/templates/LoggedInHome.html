Our Motto

Our application serves as a platform for users to Organise and Participate in events encouraging people to maintain a healthy environment. It also provides users the ability to learn about the environment around them and offers tailored advice based on the user's engagement with the application.

How can you start learning?

You can set up an account as either an "environmentalist" or an "organiser", where you will have the opportunity to take part in quizzes conducted weekly.

These quizzes will analyse your day to day habits and provide you with a score which conveys your carbon footprint.
